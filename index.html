Diagnosis of Mental Disorders.

Started

Who we are:

We are a cutting-edge startup dedicated to revolutionizing mental health diagnostics through advanced neurotechnology. Our mission is to develop innovative software that utilizes brain-computer interfaces (BCI), specifically electroencephalogram (EEG) and functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS) technologies, to detect and assess mental health disorders. By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning, we analyze real-time brain activity patterns to provide accurate and non-invasive mental health assessments. Our interdisciplinary team of experts in neuroscience, AI, and software development is committed to creating solutions that bring mental health diagnostics into the modern era, enabling early detection and personalized interventions for those in need. At the core of our startup is the belief that mental health care should be accessible, precise, and data-driven, transforming the way disorders like anxiety, depression, and other cognitive impairments are identified and treated.

What we do:

CONCUSSION

Various tests and tools are used to assess cognitive and neuropsychological changes post-concussion, as traumatic brain injury (TBI) is known to cause cognitive impairment. NIRSIT can be utilized simultaneously with the assessment tools in monitoring, real-time, the brain conditions of those that have suffered concussion and are likely to have an adverse effect, such as cognitive impairment.

MENTAL DISORDERS

Chronic depression and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) have been associated with cognitive impairments involving memory and attention. Those affected by depression and PTSD are assessed for delayed verbal and figural memory, attention, and learning. NIRSIT can be utilized simultaneously with such assessment tools to monitor the brain conditions of those suffering from chronic depression and/or PTSD.

NEURO-REHABILITATION

Neuro rehabilitation involves finding the pattern of functional strengths and weaknesses, which become the foundation for designing a program of brain rehabilitation. Various cognitive tasks are designed to help brain rehabilitation. NIRSIT can be utilized simultaneously with the cognitive tasks practices to monitor the progress of neuro rehabilitation.

EDUCATION

In general, the left and right hemispheres of our brain process information in different ways. While we have a natural tendency towards one way of thinking, the two sides of our brain work together in our everyday lives. NIRSIT can be utilized to monitor the brain activations of both the left and right hemispheres and compare the two when performing diverse cognitive as well as behavioral tasks.

ARMONI seeks to transform the approach in the study of mental health.
